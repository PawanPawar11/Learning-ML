# What is mean, and how to calculate it?

Mean is just the mathematical word for average.

In simple terms:

**Average = Mean**

People use the word mean in maths and statistics because it is more precise, but the idea is the same:

* Add all the values
* Divide by how many values there are

So whenever you hear mean, you can think average.

---

## How to calculate the mean (step by step)

* Add all the numbers
* Count how many numbers there are
* Divide the total by the count

---

## Formula

$$
\text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of all values}}{\text{Number of values}}
$$

---

## Example

Find the mean of: **2, 4, 6, 8**

### Step 1: Add the numbers

2 + 4 + 6 + 8 = 20

### Step 2: Count the numbers

There are 4 numbers

### Step 3: Divide

20 ÷ 4 = 5

✅ **Mean = 5**
